Prof. Chinua Achebe

The greatest African novelist of all time.

A brief history of the life of Achebe:

  • Full Name - Albert Chinualumogu Achebe
  • Born - November 16, 1930
  • Place of Birth - Ogidi, Anambra State
  • Country - Nigeria
  • First Novel - Things Fall Apart, 1958
  • Marriage - Married Christie Chinwe in 1961, with whom they have four children together.
  • Company - Started Citadel Press in 1967 with his colleague Christopher Okigbo, intended to serve as an outlet for a new kind of African-oriented children's books.
  • Died - March 21, 2013 at the age of 82 in Boston Massachusetts, USA.
“A man who calls his kinsmen to a feast does not do so to save them from starving. They all have food in their own homes. When we gather together in the moonlit village ground it is not because of the moon. Every man can see it in his own compound. We come together because it is good for kinsmen to do so.” ― Chinua Achebe.
